Web Development Projects
Last Resort is a social app that provides it's users with a quick solution to their drinks and music needs. Based on the responses to a selected set of questions, the app is able to offer up suggestions of a drink and music combo. Gone are the days of wondering and agonizing over "What to do tonight?". This app relieves the pressure and stress of planning a date or any other social activity. In order to successfully create this app, I used the aid of two Api's: Musixmatch and TheCocktailDB. Through these Api's, my teammates and I were able to generate the data we needed give the necessary results.
